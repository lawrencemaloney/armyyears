December 10, 1971

Fussball!

This game took on an international flavor once when out battalion was en route to our bi-annual trip to Hoenfels. We were guests of the German Army (part of the Bundeswehr formed after WW II) as a stop-over. Although there were some initial language difficulties, they were soon overcome by the international language of beer and fussball. I don't remember who won, but we held our own, on both fronts, as I recall.
