March 5, 1971

Trip to the "Zone".

Twice each year, the our battalion traveled by convoy from Berlin, through occupied East Germany, to the U. S. Army training area in Hoenfels, West Germany. Hoenfels is located 60 miles from the Czech border, which in those days was not super friendly with the U. S. Army. It was (and still is) about 60 miles southwest of Nuremberg. The trip from Berlin (about 300 miles) took three days. I was assigned as the Battalion Commander's sedan driver, so I got to drive a car while many other soldiers were in trucks, some of them in the back sitting on wooden benches. What fun!.
